## 1. Brand Identity & Positioning

**Brand Personality:**
- **Edgy**: Dark theme, graffiti-style logo, street culture

- **Accessible**: Easy to use, Thai language, inclusive

**Brand Values:**
- **Authenticity**: 100% genuine products
- **Security**: Safe transactions, buyer protection
- **Community**: Connecting buyers and sellers

## 8. Risk Mitigation

### 8.1 Potential Challenges

**Challenge 1: Competition**
- **Risk**: Established platforms (Shopee, Lazada)
- **Mitigation**: Focus on niche (street culture), differentiate with unique brand

**Challenge 2: Trust**
- **Risk**: New platform, unknown brand
- **Mitigation**: Emphasize security, showcase reviews, money-back guarantee

**Challenge 3: Supply**
- **Risk**: Not enough sellers/products
- **Mitigation**: Aggressive seller recruitment, incentives, support

**Challenge 4: Demand**
- **Risk**: Not enough buyers
- **Mitigation**: Strong marketing, referral program, promotions

---

### 8.2 Contingency Plans

**If user acquisition is slow:**
- Increase ad spend
- Offer bigger discounts
- Partner with influencers
- Run viral campaigns

**If seller acquisition is slow:**
- Extend zero-commission period
- Offer cash incentives
- Provide more support
- Simplify onboarding

**If retention is low:**
- Improve product quality
- Enhance customer service
- Add loyalty rewards
- Personalize experience
